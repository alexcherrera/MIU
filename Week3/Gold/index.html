    About

    The purpose of this application is to have a sense of balance for the typical college student between school, work, and personal activities. By using different types of methods suchs as calendar's, todo list, reminder, and more the student will be able to organize all activites on just one device.

    Categories

    • Course Information

      This calendar is designed mainly for a college student but can be used by everone. Record information about your course, such as your professor's name, start and last day of class, the time class starts and finsihes, and much more.

    • Course Assignment

      Now you have the ability to add asignments for each class, add when recieved, when the due date is, notes, and more.

    • Events

      Be able to add all your college events such as activities for clubs, sororities, fraternities, and more.

    • Remiders

      All your reminders related to courses or anything in your college will be in this section.

    • Todo List

      All your todo's for your college life will be created and stored in this section.
